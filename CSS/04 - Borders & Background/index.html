What is lorem ipsum? Lorem ipsum, sometimes called "lipsum," is a form of placeholder text that mimics the natural visual flow of content in a space. The phrase "lorem ipsum" derives from the Latin phrase "dolorem ipsum," which translates to "pain itself." Lorem ipsum text often appears to readers as a segment of Latin text, but the text actually uses a scrambled mixture of Latin elements and made-up words or phases. The placeholder text is meaningless, and designers in print and digital industries use it as it allows them to focus on the design of the project instead of the content.
